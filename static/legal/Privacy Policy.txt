Your privacy is important to us. It is Udia's policy to be operationally transparent, while respecting your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our websites, https://udia.ca, https://api.udia.ca, and other sites we own and operate (collectively the "Service").

No personally identifying information is shared publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law.

You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with your desired services.

Your continued use of our service will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information.
